Planes

By Alex

This page is an overview of my website. it is about one of my passions, planes. The following images (except one) are taken by myself.

The contents of this website contain the following: two types of planes, small paragraphs about them and a small description about the images, along with a final page containing the history of planes and their uses.


I got interested in the aviation world at the start of 2024 when I went on holiday in the South Island. before that, I hadn't been on a plane since 2018 so I was very hyped and optimistic. After this, my eagerness to see planes eventually led to me going planespotting. It is a very fun activity (although quite long) if you're a aviation enthusiast and especially if you like loud noises, great photo opportunities, and just being in the moment.
